# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-04*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Bring the showroom to your driveway with Northwest Indiana's top-rated mobile detailing setup.
**What it does:** Provides professional, zero-hassle mobile car detailing services directly at the customer's home. Services include interior restoration, exterior detailing, and ceramic coatings.
**Product category:** Mobile Auto Detailing Service
**Product type:** Local Service Business
**Business model:** Pay-per-service (with tiered packages: Essential, Restoration, Exterior) and high-ticket add-ons (Ceramic Coating).

## Target Audience
**Target customers:** Busy families, professionals, and car enthusiasts in Northwest Indiana (NWI).
**Decision-makers:** Car owners (typically the household schedule manager or the primary driver).
**Primary use case:** Restoring a vehicle's appearance without the inconvenience of driving to and waiting at a shop.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Deep clean a heavily used family vehicle (remove stains, odors, crumbs).
- Protect and shine the exterior of a vehicle (wax, ceramic coating).
- Save time by having the service come to them.
**Use cases:**
- Preparing a car for sale.
- Seasonal deep cleaning (post-winter or pre-summer).
- Correcting specific issues (spills, pet hair, off-road mud).

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** People want a clean car but don't have the time or energy to do it themselves, nor do they want to coordinate drop-offs and pick-ups at a physical detail shop.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- *Automatic car washes:* Scratch paint, miss the interior, don't remove embedded dirt.
- *Physical detail shops:* Inconvenient scheduling, requires arranging rides, no personal guarantee.
- *DIY at home:* Takes hours, lacks professional-grade tools (steamers, extractors) and results.
**What it costs them:** Time on their weekends, pride in their vehicle's appearance, potential resale value.
**Emotional tension:** Frustration with a messy car, guilt over not maintaining it, stress of finding time to clean it.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other local mobile detailers — might lack professional branding, a structured booking process, or the 100+ 5-star review proof.
**Secondary:** Brick-and-mortar detail shops — falls short because it requires the customer to travel and wait.
**Indirect:** Drive-through car washes — falls short because it damages paint and neglects the interior.

## Differentiation

- Run by Noah Miller, an IU student/Community Manager building a brand on trust and hard work.

**How we do it differently:** We prioritize the customer's time and peace of mind by handling everything on-site with zero demands on their home utilities.
**Why that's better:** True "zero-hassle" experience.
**Why customers choose us:** Trust (emphasized by the founder bio and heavy social proof), convenience, and proven high-quality results.

**Anti-persona:** Bargain hunters looking for a quick $20 wipe-down; people outside the NWI service radius.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Driving around in a filthy car, embarrassment giving people a ride, spilling something that smells.
**Pull:** Seeing before/after photos of similar cars, reading glowing reviews, the appeal of not having to leave the house.
**Habit:** Running it through the local tunnel wash out of convenience.
**Anxiety:** Worried the detailer won't show up, worried the price will change on-site, concerned about leaving a stranger in their driveway.
